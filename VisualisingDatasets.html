About my Visualising Techniques in Detail

A technique to use the facts to tell a beautiful story from the data

I have experience in visualizing data using a variety of software and programming languages such as Python, R, and Tableau. I have been able to assimilate, aggregate, and query financial data to provide recommendations based on statistical analysis.

One of my key strengths is creating multiple dashboards to showcase year-on-year analysis of different products and services. This has helped me to gain a deeper understanding of the data and identify trends and patterns that would have been difficult to spot otherwise. In addition, I have used multiple visualization methods like funnel and map visuals to study current organizational and management strategies, and compare the feasibility and potential impacts of proposed alternatives.

In my experience, Python libraries like Matplotlib and Seaborn have been invaluable tools for data visualization. They are easy to use and provide a wide range of options for creating visually appealing plots and charts. Furthermore, I have used Tableau for creating interactive dashboards and reports that can be easily shared with stakeholders.

Another tool that I have experience with is Power BI, which is a business intelligence tool that allows you to connect to various data sources and create interactive dashboards and reports. It is a great tool for data analysis and visualization, and it is also very user-friendly.
